"What about reimbursement coverage?"

Competitive Intelligence Center

UroLift (Teleflex)
Market Position:
Market leader with 35-40% share, established DTC marketing, and broad payer coverage
Key Differentiators:
Single spring vs multiple implants, no prostate size limitations, simpler deployment procedure
Pricing Strategy:
Premium pricing with established reimbursement pathways and hospital contracts
Response Strategy:
Emphasize procedural simplicity, universal applicability, and immediate symptom relief
Rezūm (Boston Scientific)
Market Position:
30-35% share with steam therapy, strong clinical evidence, office-based positioning
Key Differentiators:
Immediate vs delayed results, no tissue destruction vs thermal ablation, reversible treatment
Pricing Strategy:
Competitive procedure-based pricing with disposable delivery system
Response Strategy:
Highlight immediate symptom relief, preservation of anatomy, and reversibility
Aquablation (PROCEPT BioRobotics)
Market Position:
Robotic precision for larger prostates, hospital-based procedures, emerging technology
Key Differentiators:
Office vs OR setting, minimal vs extensive training required, broader patient eligibility
Pricing Strategy:
High-value robotic procedure pricing with capital equipment requirements
Response Strategy:
Focus on office convenience, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness
